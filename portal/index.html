Foundation & Core Values

Our operational compass. Hover or focus any card to reveal the internal execution guide.

1. Integrity

Integridad

We stand firm in transparency. We deliver exactly what is promised to both property owners and traveling guests, leaving no room for discrepancies.

How we live it

Immediate communication the moment a breakdown occurs. We refund or repair preemptively, before the guest has to ask.

2. Ownership

Responsabilidad

Extreme accountability is our baseline. Every host, VA, and operations team member owns the outcome of their actions. No excuses.

How we live it

We never tell a guest it was the cleaning crew's fault. We apologize, resolve it within 45 minutes, then fix the internal process.
