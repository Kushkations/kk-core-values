02 / Valores Fundamentales

Core Values

Six words. They apply to everyone, including Shaquan. Every decision gets checked against them. Hover or focus a card to reveal the execution guide.

01

Ownership

Responsabilidad

If we see it, we solve it. No "not my job." Every unit you touch carries your name.Si lo vemos, lo resolvemos. Nada de "no es mi trabajo". Cada unidad que tocas lleva tu nombre.

How we live it

We never tell a guest it was the cleaning crew's fault. We apologize, resolve it within 45 minutes, then fix the internal process.

02

Integrity

Integridad

Do it right even when nobody checks. An honest "not yet" beats a fake "done."Hazlo bien aunque nadie te revise. Un "todavía no" honesto vale más que un "listo" falso.

How we live it

Immediate communication the moment a breakdown occurs. We refund or repair preemptively, before the guest has to ask.

03

Persistence

Persistencia

"No" is a starting point. We find a way. (Encontramos la manera.)El "no" es un punto de partida. Encontramos la manera.

How we live it

An unfair review is not the end of the conversation. We document it, cite the policy it violates, and take it back to the platform until it is resolved.

04

Team

Equipo

Cleaners, maintenance, VAs. We win together. Nobody succeeds alone.Limpieza, mantenimiento, VAs: ganamos juntos. Nadie triunfa solo.

How we live it

A handoff is not finished until the next person can act on it. Every issue leaves the shift with an owner, not a mention.

05

Growth

Crecimiento

Better than yesterday. We don't repeat errors, we retire them.Mejor que ayer. No repetimos errores, los retiramos.

How we live it

The same mistake twice is a process problem, not a people problem. We fix the system that allowed it and write it down.

06

Hospitality

Hospitalidad

Success is measured by the guest experience. The guest feels our work.El éxito se mide por la experiencia del huésped. El huésped siente nuestro trabajo.

How we live it

Custom design themes, a warm and professional voice, and answers that come from the house manual rather than a template.

Before you leave a unit, ask: "Would I pay to stay here tonight?" · Antes de salir de una unidad: "¿Pagaría yo por quedarme aquí esta noche?"

03 / Identidad

Identity

Logos, colour, and type. If you are making something with our name on it, the rules on this page are the whole brief.

Logo usage Colour Typography Voice

Logo usage

Clear space

Keep clear space around every mark equal to at least half the height of the mark. Nothing, including type, photos, or page edges, enters that box.

Minimum size

64px OK
32px too small

The KushKations badge carries a leaf, a mountain, and circular text. Below roughly 64px tall it stops being readable. Use it at 64px or larger, or commission a simplified small-size mark.

Variants on file

What exists today, and what has to be commissioned. The packet roadmap already records this gap: "Vector (SVG/AI) versions of all logos. Only PNG/JPEG exist today."

Logo variants available per brand
Brand Full colour Vector Reversed Small size Gap
Cashaw Management PNG — — — Wordmark only. No stacked lockup for square placements.
KushKations™ PNG — — — Badge is illegible below 64px. Needs a simplified small-size mark.
All Exclusive BNB PNG — — — Art is built on a black field. Unusable on light without a reversed cut.
Same-Day BNB PNG — — — Circular text fails at small sizes. Same problem as KushKations.
Clearsite PNG — — — Mark is black. Invisible on navy. No reversed version exists.

Every gap above needs a designer, not a developer. Until a reversed or small-size cut exists, do not improvise one: ask, or use the mark at a size and on a background where it works.

Do not

  • Do not stretch, squash, or rotate a mark.No estirar, aplastar ni rotar un logo.
  • Do not recolour a mark or add effects.No cambiar el color de un logo ni agregar efectos.
  • Do not put the KushKations green badge on a green field.No poner el escudo verde de KushKations sobre fondo verde.
  • Do not rebuild a mark from scratch. Use the files in Drive.No rehacer un logo desde cero. Usa los archivos de Drive.
  • Do not write "Kush Kations" or "Kushkations".No escribir "Kush Kations" ni "Kushkations".
  • Do not use a sub-brand mark in guest-facing work until its status is confirmed.No usar un logo de submarca con huéspedes hasta confirmar su estado.

Colour rules

  • Never red or orange. Off-brand.Nunca rojo ni naranja. Fuera de marca.
  • Gold is used sparingly: core values and premium moments only.El dorado se usa con moderación: solo valores fundamentales y momentos premium.
  • Two golds exist. KK Gold #d4a944 belongs to KushKations. Cashaw Gold #c8992f is the wordmark's own. Do not mix them in one piece.

Open decision

The packet records this as undecided: cashawmanagement.com uses blue, so either Cashaw aligns to the KushKations greens, or blue becomes Cashaw's own corporate colour. This portal assumes the second: navy is Cashaw's, green stays KushKations'. Shaquan to confirm.

Two marks on file also break the "never orange" rule: Clearsite is black and orange, AEBNB carries an orange sun. Resolve alongside those brands' status.
